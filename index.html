About Us

At Elderly Aura, our mission is to enhance the lives of seniors by connecting them with dedicated volunteers and providing valuable health and wellness resources. We strive to create a supportive and engaging community for the elderly, ensuring they receive the assistance and companionship they need.

Our Services

On-Demand Assistance

Just like a rideshare service, our app notifies volunteers when a senior requests assistance. Whether it's help with grocery shopping, transportation to appointments, or household chores, our volunteers are ready to assist.

Companionship and Social Interaction

Our volunteers provide regular visits to seniors, offering companionship and social interaction to combat loneliness and improve mental well-being.

Health and Wellness Support

We offer guidance on medication management, ensuring seniors take the right medications at the right times. Our platform also provides information on various health topics to promote overall wellness. .

Yoga and Exercise Tips

Staying active is crucial for maintaining good health. Elderly Aura provides tips and tricks for yoga and other gentle exercises tailored for seniors to help them stay fit and flexible.

Event and Activity Organization

We organize community events and activities to keep seniors engaged and active. From group yoga sessions to social gatherings, we aim to create opportunities for seniors to connect and have fun.

Personalized Care Plans

We understand that every senior has unique needs. Our platform allows for the creation of personalized care plans, ensuring that each individual receives the specific support and services they require.

Why Us?

Innovative and Efficient Service

Our app functions like a rideshare service, making it easy and efficient to connect seniors with volunteers. The real-time notifications ensure that help is just a tap away.

Comprehensive Support

From daily assistance to health and wellness resources, we offer a wide range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of seniors. Our holistic approach ensures that all aspects of their well-being are addressed.

Community Engagement

We organize events and activities to keep seniors engaged and socially active, fostering a sense of community and belonging.
